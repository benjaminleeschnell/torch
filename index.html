Torch gives parents the same powers online that they have offline.

Torch is fixing problems caused by the way we access the internet when it comes to raising kids. We’re designing a router that can filter content and help schedule kids’ internet use, all with one device. We’re also giving parents visibility into how their kids use the internet in order to stimulate conversations at home. Where a child goes online says a lot about who they are and who they are becoming. Parents should feel empowered to help their kids lean into the discoveries that technology affords, not feel threatened or powerless to them.
